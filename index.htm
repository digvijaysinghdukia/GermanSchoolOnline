Yes, you can easily book an appointment through our website or by calling us.

Please bring your previous medical reports, a list of current medications, and a valid ID proof.

What happens during an appointment?

The doctor will assess your health concerns, review your medical history, and plan any necessary tests or treatments.

Our clinic is open Monday to Saturday, from 9 AM to 7 PM.
