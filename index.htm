Frequently Asked Questions

What languages do you teach?

We offer courses in German, Italian, French, and Japanese.

Who are the teachers?

Our instructors are certified professionals with extensive experience in teaching their respective languages.

What levels do you offer?

We provide courses for all levels:
Beginner (A1 & A2)
Intermediate (B1 & B2)
Advanced (C1 & C2)

What is the class schedule?

Our classes are flexible and run in the mornings, afternoons, evenings, and weekends to accommodate all schedules.

What is the duration of a course?

A1 to B1 course lasts approximately 4–5 months, depending on the level and intensity of the program.

Do you offer private lessons?

Yes, we offer one-on-one lessons tailored to individual needs and goals.

What is the class size?

We keep our classes small, with a maximum of 10 students, to ensure personalized attention.

Do you have a trial lesson?

Yes, we offer a free trial lesson so you can experience our teaching methods before enrolling.
